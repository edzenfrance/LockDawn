This is the pro version of our Mall-Scene.
The package comes with parts of our city-buildings-,
playground- and pine-tree-collection.
The whole broadleave trees package and VISky (sky-system)
is also included in this package.

For more information about VISky have a look at the readme
in the VISky Folder.

Collision meshes for ground and walls are also
included.

Texture sizes between 512x512 to 4096x1024.
